About Us

An NGO, or Non-Governmental Organization, is a group that works independently from the government to address social, environmental, or humanitarian issues. NGOs can be small local groups or large international organizations. They focus on various causes, such as helping the poor, protecting the environment, promoting human rights, and providing education and healthcare. They are often funded by donations and grants, and their goal is to make a positive impact on society.
